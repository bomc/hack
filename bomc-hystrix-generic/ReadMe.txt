Dashboard:

The library "hystrix-dashboard-1.5.9.war" must be copied, deployed in wildfly deployment directory.

Invoke the hystrix-dashboard in webbrowser: "hystrix-dashboard-1.5.9"

- Use for simple case, 'Stream Type: Hystrix'
- Enter the url: '192.168.4.1:8180/bomc-hystrix-generic/hystrix.stream'

And than click "Add Stream"-button.

And than click monitor streams.
